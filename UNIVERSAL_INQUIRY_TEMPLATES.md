# Universal Business Inquiry Templates - Works for ANY Industry

## ✅ WHAT CHANGED

**Updated automation to use UNIVERSAL inquiries** that work for any type of company or industry.

### Before (Industry-Specific)
```
I'm reaching out to inquire about home building from your company...
I'm planning a home building project...
My family and I are looking to build a new home...
```
❌ Only worked for construction/home building companies

### After (Universal)
```
I'm reaching out to inquire about your services...
I'm planning a project and wanted to reach out...
I came across your company and would like to learn more...
```
✅ Works for ANY type of company in ANY industry

## Why Universal Templates?

**Problem**: You're sending to many different companies across various industries
- Restaurants
- Retail stores
- Service providers
- Manufacturing
- Technology companies
- Healthcare
- Real estate
- etc.

**Solution**: Generic business inquiry language that applies to everyone

## 12 Universal Inquiry Variations

### Style 1: General Service Inquiry
```
Hello,

I'm reaching out to inquire about your services. I'd appreciate 
any information on availability, pricing, and what you offer.

Please let me know the best way to proceed or if you need 
additional details from me.

Thank you,
John Smith
```

### Style 2: Project-Based
```
Hi there,

I'm interested in discussing a potential project with your company. 
Could you provide information about your current availability and 
pricing structure?

I'd also like to know about typical timelines and what options 
you can accommodate.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,
John Smith
```

### Style 3: Direct and Brief
```
Good morning,

I'm considering working with your company and would like to learn 
more about your services. What information would you need from me 
to provide a quote?

Please advise on next steps and approximate timelines.

Thanks,
John Smith
```

### Style 4: Detailed Inquiry
```
Hello,

I'm writing to inquire about your services. I'm particularly 
interested in understanding:

- Current availability
- Typical timelines
- Pricing ranges and payment terms
- Available options

Please let me know how we can move forward with this discussion.

Best,
John Smith
```

### Style 5: Personal Approach
```
Hi,

I came across your company and would like to learn more about 
what you offer. Would you be able to share information about 
your services and availability?

I'd appreciate learning about your process, pricing, and what 
you'd need from me to get started.

Thank you for your time.

John Smith
```

### Style 6: Timeline Focused
```
Hello,

I'm planning a project and wanted to reach out about your services. 
What are your current lead times?

Also, could you provide information on pricing and what 
specifications you typically work with?

Appreciate your help.

Regards,
John Smith
```

### Style 7: Research Phase
```
Good afternoon,

I'm in the early stages of planning and gathering information from 
different providers. Could you share details about your services, 
including availability and pricing?

Any information about your process and timelines would be helpful 
as well.

Thanks in advance,
John Smith
```

### Style 8: Recommendation-Based
```
Greetings,

I was referred to your company and would like to learn more about 
your current availability and what you offer.

Could you provide information on pricing, options, and typical 
project timelines?

Looking forward to your response.

John Smith
```

### Style 9: Budget Conscious
```
Hello,

I'm interested in your services and would like to understand your 
pricing structure and what's included. What information do you need 
from me to provide an estimate?

Also curious about your availability.

Thank you,
John Smith
```

### Style 10: Comparison Shopping
```
Hi,

I'm comparing several providers and wanted to learn more about your 
company. Could you provide details on your services, availability, 
and pricing?

I'd also appreciate information about your offerings and timelines.

Best regards,
John Smith
```

### Style 11: Ready to Move Forward
```
Good morning,

I'm ready to move forward with a project and would like to discuss 
your services. What are your current lead times and availability?

Please share information about pricing and what you'd need from me 
to begin the process.

Thanks,
John Smith
```

### Style 12: Exploratory
```
Hello,

I'm exploring options and would like to learn more about what you 
offer. Could you provide an overview of your services, including 
typical timelines and pricing ranges?

I'd also like to know what options and packages you have available.

Appreciate your help.

Regards,
John Smith
```
