### **Step 2: Add URLs**
1. Scroll to "URL Rotation" section

3. Click "Add" button
4. Repeat for 3-5 URLs

**Example URLs:**
- https://yoursite1.com/invoice
- https://yoursite2.com/payment
- https://yoursite3.com/portal

### **Step 3: Add Email Batches**
1. Scroll to "Add Email Batch" section
2. Paste emails (one per line) in the textarea:
   ```
   customer1@example.com
   customer2@example.com
   customer3@example.com
   ```
3. Fill in:
   - Work Order: `WO-12345`
   - Reference: `REF-67890`
   - Service: `HVAC Maintenance`
   - Due Date: Select from calendar
   - Contact Email: `support@yourcompany.com`
4. Click "Add to Queue"

### **Step 4: Monitor Status**
- **System Status** shows if automation is running
- **Queue Count** shows how many emails are waiting
- **Last Send** shows when last batch was sent
- **Next Send** shows estimated next send time

### **Step 5: Control Automation**
- Click **"RESUME"** to start automation
- Click **"PAUSE"** to stop automation
- Click **"SEND NOW"** to force immediate send
- Click **"REFRESH"** to update all stats
